Tim Frost

I’m Tim Frost. I make animation and games from a shed in West Sussex, UK. I like drawing, trees, birds, hats, computers, haiku and bicycles. I’m a freelancer and I’ve worked on a wide range of animation and game projects over the years (Peppa Pig, Moshi Monsters, Hey Duggee!, Teach Your Monster).

I also work on my own animation and game projects when time allows. I like to use FOSS (Free and Open Source Software) tools whenever I can, and I’m increasingly adding them to my professional workflow. Tools such as Blender, Godot, Krita, Inkscape and Linux. I know, right?
